# Holy Infusion

You invigorate an attacking ally with divine energy.

- **Keywords:** Magic, Ranged
- **Type:** Triggered
- **Distance:** Ranged 5
- **Target:** 1 ally

**Effect:** The attack gains an edge and deals holy damage equal to twice your Intuition score.
