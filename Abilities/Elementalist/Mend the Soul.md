# Mend the Soul

The power you channel grants the ability to get back in the fight.

- **Keywords:** Green, Magic, Ranged
- **Type:** Triggered
- **Distance:** Ranged 5
- **Target:** Self or 1 ally
- **Trigger:** The target starts their turn.

**Effect:** The target can spend a Recovery.
